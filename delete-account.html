Account Deletion – FocusPort

If you want to delete your account and associated data from FocusPort, please follow the steps below:

  • Send an email to: focusport.support@gmail.com
  • Use the subject: Data Deletion Request - FocusPort
We will process valid requests within 7 days.

All associated personal data (such as name, email address, usage data, and Firebase user ID) will be permanently deleted from our servers.

You may also delete local data by signing out and clearing app data from your device.
