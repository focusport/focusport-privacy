// About FocusPort

THE APP BEHIND THE LOCK.

FocusPort is a free Android productivity app designed to block distracting apps during focus sessions. Unlike apps like Forest, Freedom, or StayFocused that rely on willpower or gamification, FocusPort uses Android Accessibility Service to make blocking bypass-proof. When a session starts, the apps you selected are truly inaccessible — no back gestures, no home button escape, no "just five more minutes" button.

It was built for students who keep opening Instagram during study time, remote workers who switch to YouTube mid-task, and anyone who's tired of doomscrolling when they meant to send one message. The app doesn't lock your whole phone — calls, messages, and emergency apps you whitelist stay available — but everything else waits until the timer ends.

FocusPort includes scheduled sessions (so your phone locks automatically at 9 AM every weekday), detailed session history, and a streak system that breaks if you quit early. There's no pretending. You either finish the session or your streak resets. It's honest accountability that actually works.

Available on Google Play. Works offline. No account required. Install for free.
