1. Overview

FocusPort ("we", "our", or "the app") is a focus and productivity app built for Android. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is stored and protected. We are committed to being fully transparent about our data practices.

FocusPort is operated by an independent developer. For the purposes of applicable data protection laws, FocusPort acts as the data controller of your personal data.

🛡️ Guest Mode: If you use FocusPort as a guest (without signing in), no data ever leaves your device. All session data is stored locally in your phone's private app storage only.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Account Information: Name and email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Usage Data: Focus sessions, duration, completion status, streaks
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and app usage logs for debugging and performance improvements.
  • Log Data: In case of app errors, we may collect diagnostic information such as crash logs to improve stability.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as passwords or financial information.

👤 Guest Users ON-DEVICE ONLY
  • Focus session history (duration, completion status, timestamps)
  • Current streak and best streak count
  • Emergency app preferences
  • App settings (dark mode, scheduled programs)

None of this data leaves your device. It is stored in Android SharedPreferences, private to this app only.

🔑 Signed-In Users (Google Account) SYNCED TO CLOUD
  • Your Google Account display name and anonymous Firebase UID
  • Focus session records (start time, duration, completion status)
  • Streak count and best streak
  • Total sessions completed and total focus minutes
  • Exit key balance (number of keys available)
  • Last completed session date
  • Premium status and trial expiry timestamp
  • Migration flags (internal — whether guest data has been transferred to your account)

Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore and is associated with a Firebase UID. While your Google account is used for authentication, your focus data is stored separately and is not publicly linked to your identity.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Track your focus sessions and progress
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide features such as streak tracking and statistics

4. How We Use the Accessibility Service

⚠️ FocusPort uses Android's Accessibility Service only to detect when you leave the app during an active focus session, and to help enforce your focus session by restricting distractions — a session you have personally configured and started.

The Accessibility Service is used strictly for this single purpose. It does not read text from your screen, interact with other apps on your behalf, collect or store any data from other apps, or operate when no focus session is active. You can revoke this permission at any time via Android Settings → Accessibility.

5. How We Use Device Administrator

FocusPort requests Device Administrator permission solely to lock the screen during active focus sessions initiated by the user. It is not used to wipe your device, enforce password policies, monitor device usage, or perform any action outside of an active, user-initiated focus session.

6. Exit Keys — In-App Purchases

FocusPort offers "Exit Keys" as consumable in-app items. Users can always choose to end a session at any time, which may result in loss of streak progress. Exit Keys are used to leave the session without completing it fully.

  • Your first Exit Key is granted free after completing your first focus session
  • Additional keys are available for purchase via Google Play Billing
  • Your key balance is stored in Firebase (signed-in) or locally (guest)
  • Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play — All payments are processed by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information.

7. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud services). We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will take appropriate steps to notify affected users as required by applicable laws.

Your data may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country, including by Google Cloud services.

8. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These include:

  • Google Sign-In (for authentication)
  • Firebase (Firestore, Authentication)

These services may collect data such as your email address, usage data, and device information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Google Firebase (Firestore & Authentication)

Used for cloud data storage and Google Sign-In. Governed by the Google Firebase Privacy Policy.

Google Play Billing

Used to process Exit Key purchases. Governed by the Google Payments Privacy Notice.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you stop using the app or request deletion, your data will be removed within a reasonable time.

ℹ️ How to delete your data: Currently, FocusPort does not provide an in-app data deletion option. You may request deletion by contacting us at the email below. We will process valid requests within a reasonable timeframe.

You may also delete your data by signing out and clearing app data from your device. For complete account deletion from our servers, please contact us.

If you sign in with Google, uninstalling the app does not automatically delete your cloud data. You must contact us to request full deletion from our servers.
